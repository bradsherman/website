A little bit about me...

I am a member of the class of 2018 at Notre Dame majoring in Computer Science. I am from Ida, a small town in south eastern Michigan. I came to Notre Dame as a chemical engineer, but started coding in the second semester of my freshman year, switched to computer science, and never looked back. I am always trying to learn more about programming and computers in general. I am especially interested in networking and cyber security. I believe that now more than ever we need to focus on security because soon the "Internet of Things" will surround us, and we must make sure that any item that has a computer in it is secure.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy playing sports. I played basketball and baseball in high school, so I try to play those as much as I can. I also love throwing a football around, or trying out new sports like volleyball or soccer. I'm a Detroit sports fan, and I enjoy watching the Tigers, Lions, Red Wings, and Pistons when I have time. When I'm not playing sports or doing schoolwork, I enjoy watching movies and playing video games with my friends. I am also interested in working on side projects, but since I'm relatively new to computer science, I am learning as much as I can in order to sharpen my skills.

Extracurricular Activities

When I started programming at the beginning of my sophomore year at Notre Dame, we did all of our projects on clusters that ran Redhat Linux. I had never heard of linux before, so I did some research on it, and by the time I came back for my second semester, I had dual booted my computer with Windows and Ubuntu. Ever since, 90% of the time I am on my computer, I use Ubuntu. I like to spend time learning about many things like window managers, raspberry pi's, and other things related to Linux operating systems. From this interest, I joined the Linux user group at Notre Dame, or ND Lug. We are a brand new club, but some of our topics have included text editors, window managers (which caused me to start using the i3wm), and raspberry pi projects.

One of the things I am most passionate about is sports. As I said before I played baseball and basketball in high school, but I enjoy many other sports like football and hockey as well. I play intramural baseball and basketball. Our team won the intramural championship this past year for baseball. I also played in the Bookstore Basketball tournament at Notre Dame in the spring of 2016. Bookstore basketball is the world's largest outdoor 5 on 5 basketball tournament. Our team finished second out of around 600 teams. I also enjoy watching movies and tv shows like Game of Thrones and Silicon Valley, which are two of my favorite shows right now.
